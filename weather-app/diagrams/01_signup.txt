title 1. User Signup

participant Frontend
participant FastAPI
participant SQLite

Frontend->FastAPI: POST /signup\n{username, password, address}
FastAPI->SQLite: SELECT 1 FROM users\nWHERE username = ?
SQLite-->FastAPI: null (not found)
FastAPI->FastAPI: PBKDF2-SHA256(password, salt)\n200,000 iterations
FastAPI->SQLite: INSERT INTO users\n(username, password_hash, salt, address)
SQLite-->FastAPI: OK
FastAPI-->Frontend: 200 {ok: true}

note over FastAPI,SQLite: On duplicate username → 400\nOn missing fields → 400
